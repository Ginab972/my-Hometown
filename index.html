Historical Places

The Libery Bell is one of the most visited locations. It's located at Independence Hall where in 1776, the Declaration of Independence was signed. Eleven Years later the United States Constitution was created and signed in the same room.

Cultural Places

The Philadelphia Museum of Art is one of the most visited musemus, which holds more than 2,000 years of paintings, sculpture, decorative arts and architectural settings from Europe, Asia and the Americas.
Fun fact ~ The Rocky Statue is located here.
